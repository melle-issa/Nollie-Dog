Meet the Members!

Rene Arroyo:

Rene got his start in the band in 2020.
He started playing the drums and has
since taken up the shaker when necessary
for acoustic performances. Aside from
percussion, Rene also plays the bass,
guitar, ukulele, and for a short time,
the trumpet.

Angel Gomez:

Angel is the band's singer and lead guitar.
He founded the band in 2020 as a
way to continue his passion for music.
Aside from singing and guitar, Angel also
writes the lyrics to Nollie Dog's songs
and manages the band's gigs.

Nick Gomez:

Nick plays second guitar for the band and
lead guitar in Nollie Dog's spin off band
Nollie Puppy. He also acts as the band's
stylist for gigs. He and Angel are brothers
with Nick being the youngest of the two.

Jayden Edwards:

Jayden is a part time member of the band.
Initially starting off full time, he has since
stepped back to focus on school. While
in the band, he plays bass and can be seen
using a literal drill on the instrument
to create the band's unique sound during gigs.
